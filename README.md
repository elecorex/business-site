## About Elecorex
Elecorex is a global electronics supplier committed to delivering reliable, high-quality electronic components to engineers, makers, and businesses worldwide.
 Our name comes from “Electronic Core Excellence,” representing our mission to provide stable, precise, and trustworthy components that power innovation in every industry.

From microcontrollers to power modules and industrial ICs, Elecorex ensures every component meets strict quality standards. With a strong supply chain and engineering-driven culture, we help customers reduce risk, shorten development cycles, and build products with confidence.

At Elecorex, we believe that reliability is the foundation of technology—and we are proud to be the core your projects can depend on.
